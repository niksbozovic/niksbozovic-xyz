HARTIJA (2023-24)

Hartija was a zine publishing project, collecting poetry, short stories and photography by young artists, and sharing them with the masses. This was done through an open call for their artworks, where over 50 people signed up, later selecting 9 artists in various fields, a pop up exhibition and poetry night and a series of 9 zines printed on a risograph.

This project was a collaboration with Milena Stepanović and Miloš Mijanović. You can view the full project at hartija.xyz.
