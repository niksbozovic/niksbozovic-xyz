KRUG DVOJKE (2023)

Krug Dvojke is a project researching the political graffiti in the center of Belgrade. This tram route was selected because it circles the main streets of Belgrade and it raises the question: if these are the messages the center of Belgrade sends, what does the rest of Serbia tell us? This project is also an archive of dDojka, all the found interventions on graffiti of war criminals and what happens when they aren’t hidden. This project was tasked with answering the question of representation of different ideologies and the mythomania when it comes to the wars of the 90s and the current war in Ukraine.

This project exists as an archive of the way in which street culture layers politics in a wider network of nationalist systems. It exists as a collection of nearly 250 photos mapped across an entire wall at the annual student exhibition in 2023.

You can view the full project at bit.ly/krug-dvojke.
